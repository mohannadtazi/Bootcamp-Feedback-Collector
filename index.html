Bootcamp Feedback Collector

Gathering invaluable insights from our bootcamp students

About the Tool

The Bootcamp Feedback Collector is an automated agent designed to reach out to students after their bootcamp experience to gather valuable feedback. It helps us learn what went well and what could be improved.

The tool classifies responses into four main categories:

  • Positive Feedback: Students share what they enjoyed.
  • Negative Feedback: Students express what didn't work or what could be improved.
  • Inquiries: Students ask questions about the bootcamp experience.
  • None: Off-topic or unclear responses.

How It Works

The agent starts by asking students about their overall bootcamp experience. Based on their response, an intent classifier categorizes the feedback and the agent follows up accordingly:

  • Positive Feedback: Follow-up questions request specific details about what was enjoyed.
  • Negative Feedback: The agent asks for more details on challenges encountered.
  • Inquiries: Provides direct responses using a dedicated knowledge base.
  • None: Guides the student back on track with clarifying questions.

Integration & Customization

Easily integrate this tool into your existing system. You can tailor the conversational prompts, adjust the intent classification logic, and even integrate with phone or chat platforms for real-time feedback collection.
